About Animal Detection Dashboard
Welcome to the Animal Detection Dashboard, an advanced platform designed to leverage cutting-edge technologies in computer vision and deep learning for detecting animals in images and videos. Our mission is to provide an intuitive and efficient tool for wildlife researchers, enthusiasts, and conservationists to monitor and study animal behavior and population.
Aims and Objectives
Our primary aims and objectives are:
  • To develop an easy-to-use interface for uploading and processing images and videos for animal detection.
  • To utilize state-of-the-art deep learning models for accurate and real-time animal detection.
  • To assist wildlife researchers and conservationists in monitoring animal activity and behavior.
  • To provide a tool that helps in the conservation efforts by identifying and tracking endangered species.
  • To enhance the understanding of animal population dynamics through detailed analysis and reporting.
